About Us

"The tour began with a visit to the historic Lapu-Lapu Shrine in Mactan, where students learned about the shrine's significance and its monuments to Lapu-Lapu and Ferdinand Magellan. They then explored PAG-ASA Cebu, gaining insights into typhoon forecasting and equipment. At FIZZBUZZ Incorporation, students discovered the art of creating stop-motion animation. The second day started at White Brick Creative Studio, where students learned about transforming projects into creative masterpieces. They then visited MICS, observing a system for tracking employee work hours, followed by the Mandaue Command Center, which demonstrated advanced surveillance and response systems. On the final day, students toured the University of San Carlos' Computer Science Department and saw cutting-edge computer units and capstone projects. The tour ended at NG KHAI Inc., where experts discussed data storage and protection using firewalls.
